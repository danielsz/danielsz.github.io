--- title: Homebrew formula for Grenchman layout: post tags: clojure grenchman homebrew Mac_OS_X categories: clojure grenchman homebrew Mac_OS_X ---

The source-only policy of the Homebrew package management system will most likely prevent the merging of a Homebrew formula for Grenchman. But fear not, a brew tap formula is available, getting you up to speed with Grenchman on Mac OS X. 

brew tap danielsz/grenchman
brew install grenchman

grench should now be on your path, ready to invoke Clojure code fast.
